Sofi (Sofia Zaiceva) is a Latvian sound artist and performer. With a background that spans classical piano training and electroacoustic composition to live coding and DJ scene, she has developed a distinctive approach to experimental electronic music that prioritises open- source approach and immersive sonic experiences. Through creative coding, spatial sound design, and improvisational practice, she excavates the relational qualities of sonic environments - revealing how digital processes can articulate deeply human experiences.
Her latest releases include collaborative album "SHE HAS CANDY" by duo CATPURSE (SOVN Records, 2025) and solo albums "Hypergloss"(Superpang 2025) and "aze" (Riforma, 2024). Sofi has been an active performer on Baltic scene, but also shared her performances across European platforms including "Musikprotokoll" (Austria), "MeetFactory" (Czech Republic), "Panke Culture" (Germany) etc. Sofi is an artist of the SHAPE+ platform (2024/2025) for innovative music and interdisciplinary art.
