about

Sofia Zaiceva is a Latvian sound artist and performer. With a background spanning classical piano training and electroacoustic composition to live coding, she has developed a distinctive approach to experimental electronic music that prioritises open-source praxis and immersive sonic experiences.

Through creative coding, spatial sound design, and improvisational practice, she excavates the relational qualities of sonic environments — revealing how digital processes can articulate deeply human experiences.

Her latest releases include the collaborative album "SHE HAS CANDY" by duo CATPURSE (SØVN Records, 2025), solo album "Hypergloss" (Superpang, 2025), and "aze" (Riforma, 2024). Sofi is an artist of the SHAPE+ platform for innovative music and interdisciplinary art (2024/2025).

Education

  • Sibelius Academy — MA Music Technology, 2023–2025
  • Estonian Academy of Music — BA Electroacoustic Composition, 2020–2023
  • Royal Conservatory Antwerp — Erasmus+, 2022–2023
  • Jāzeps Vītols Academy — BA Piano Performance, 2018–2022
